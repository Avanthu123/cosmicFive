Direct Imaging of Exoplanets: "Exoplanet Photoshoot"

In a stunning cosmic twist, JWST managed to snap a direct photo of the exoplanet HIP 65426b! It’s like the planet finally got its influencer moment, complete with glam shots. Now we can study it in detail like it’s the latest celebrity scandal. Welcome to a new era of exoplanet research, where we’ll be critiquing planets for their “outfits” and wondering why they haven’t returned our DMs. Who knew the universe was so into selfies?
