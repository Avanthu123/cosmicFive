First Deep Field Image: "Galactic Crowd Surfing"

Well, well, well, look who decided to show up! The JWST just dropped its first deep field image, revealing thousands of galaxies, like it's the ultimate cosmic reunion. Seriously, it's like the universe held a “Bring Your Own Galaxy” party and forgot to send us the invite. Now astronomers are having an existential crisis trying to understand how all these galaxies are forming while we can't even figure out our group chats!
