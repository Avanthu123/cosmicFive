Discovery of the Oldest Galaxies: "Ancient Light Show"

Surprise! JWST spotted light from galaxies that are older than your grandma’s favorite recipe—over 13 billion years old! These ancient cosmic relics are helping scientists piece together the early universe like a jigsaw puzzle missing half the pieces. It’s like finding out that your ancestors were around during the Big Bang, and now you have to justify why you can’t even keep a houseplant alive.
