Detailed Study of a Dying Star: "Star's Farewell Party"

The JWST has captured the Southern Ring Nebula, showing a dying star shedding its layers like a dramatic diva at the end of a performance. It’s a cosmic farewell party complete with intricate details, giving us insights into the life cycles of stars. If only we could throw a retirement bash for the star—complete with cake and heartfelt speeches about its stellar past!
